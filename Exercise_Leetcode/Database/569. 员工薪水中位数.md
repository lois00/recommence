### 569. 员工薪水中位数
SQL架构

Employee 表包含所有员工。Employee 表有三列：员工Id，公司名和薪水。
```
+-----+------------+--------+
|Id   | Company    | Salary |
+-----+------------+--------+
|1    | A          | 2341   |
|2    | A          | 341    |
|3    | A          | 15     |
|4    | A          | 15314  |
|5    | A          | 451    |
|6    | A          | 513    |
|7    | B          | 15     |
|8    | B          | 13     |
|9    | B          | 1154   |
|10   | B          | 1345   |
|11   | B          | 1221   |
|12   | B          | 234    |
|13   | C          | 2345   |
|14   | C          | 2645   |
|15   | C          | 2645   |
|16   | C          | 2652   |
|17   | C          | 65     |
+-----+------------+--------+
```
请编写SQL查询来查找每个公司的薪水中位数。挑战点：你是否可以在不使用任何内置的SQL函数的情况下解决此问题。
```
+-----+------------+--------+
|Id   | Company    | Salary |
+-----+------------+--------+
|5    | A          | 451    |
|6    | A          | 513    |
|12   | B          | 234    |
|9    | B          | 1154   |
|14   | C          | 2645   |
+-----+------------+--------+
```

### Solutions:
思路：


```
SELECT T.Id,T.Company,T.Salary FROM (
    SELECT t1.Id,t1.Company,t1.Salary,
            case when t2.cnt % 2 = 1 then 
                case when t1.rn = (t2.cnt/2)+1 then 1 else 0 end
            else
                case when t1.rn = t2.cnt/2 or t1.rn = (t2.cnt/2)+1 then 1 else 0 end
            end as flag
    FROM (
        SELECT *,row_number() over(partition by Company order by Salary) rn
        FROM Employee
    ) t1
    LEFT JOIN (
        SELECT Company,count(1) cnt
        FROM Employee
        GROUP BY Company
    ) t2 on t1.Company=t2.Company
) T
WHERE T.flag=1
```
